PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Overview

Topic

The project is aimed to help users and provide them with support they need to address and seek help regarding any issues regarding their mental health and wellbeing and aims to be a stepping-stone or a first step to alleviating it. The project will consist of both a mobile and web application that would appeal to all age groups, focusing on app usability and efficiency to retrieve, store, track, and analyze user data. The app will be a professional online counselling platform that provides a low-cost alternative to face-to-face counselling through smartphones, personal computers, or tablets alike. Both the mobile and web apps will be a platform where groups and individuals can seek help and track their disparities in countries around the world. Like professional counselling websites like BetterHelp.com and Talkspace.com, this project aims to help individuals who may be suffering from mental health issues, as well as reducing the mental health disparities in countries around the world.

With our planned project focusing on helping users get help they need, in regards to a sense of personalization as a feature, its likely outcome is that it would attract and appeal more to users. As our project takes it a step further in user personalization and allows our services to be different and varied and unique with each user, it would allow our project to be recommended to people and as more and more users use our service means that what we have done behind the scenes in our project is working and have successfully served its main purpose to help our users with their mental health and make a difference.

Motivation

The motivation for this project stems from the pressing issue of mental health problems and issues that impacts people in this day and age where exposure to social media is prevalent. This project is important because mental illness has long been something that many people have been trying to combat, especially since the rise of technological advances and use such as smartphones and computers. In relation to the current coronavirus pandemic, more people are staying home more than ever, albeit with their family, friends or by themselves. With staying at home, people’s activities on social media and online platforms and increasing, meaning people have more exposure to screen time. According to the Journal of Medicine, the effects of public health emergencies on individuals and communities such as confusion, emotional isolation, and owing to economic loss “may translate into a range of emotional reactions”, such as distress and psychiatric conditions (Pfefferbaum and North 2-2-, p.510). Furthermore, it Is estimated that 20.1 percent of Australians had a “mental or behavioral condition” in 2017 to 2018 (Cook 2019, para. 6). In this current digital age and IT trends, applications that aim to provide users with help and services regarding mental health issues such as Code Blue that aims to provide teenagers who struggle with bullying, anxiety, and other similar applications that has a feature of using location services in order to suggest and provide support groups. If we were able to work on this project and produce a working application, it would show a future employer on how as developers, not only does it show how we made a complex application where every feature serves an important function but also the thought behind why we made the application where it makes an impact to an important issue and would help users who need it in a positive way.

Landscape

There are a limited number of products available within the online mental health sphere, some businesses within this niche include BetterHealth, GetMosh and Online Mental Health Online. In addition, there are existing mobile applications such as MY3, notOK, and Moodlift to name a few. These competitor applications that are mentioned have one feature that are common and it utilizes GPS and location services that allows users to send their current location to support groups or chosen friends or trusted contacts if they need someone to talk to. What sets it apart from other competitors or similar products and applications is that our platform differs from these listed as it allows for the user to input their wants and needs via questionnaire or survey which will then set the user up with a suitable mental health professional and also enables users to find what specific services they need. This is done to allow for the users to have greater access to help and comfortability while using our platform. Furthermore, this survey allows users to be paired with the ideal psychologist for them as seeking help from a psychologist and booking periodic schedules is a commitment and finding the right psychologist for a user where he or she is comfortable with and the users trust. This allows our application to go an extra mile to allow a sense of personalization for our users as they get the help that they need.
