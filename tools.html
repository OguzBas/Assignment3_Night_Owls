Tools

Tools Used

Google Document - Google Document was used as a platform for the group to compile the information required for assignment 3. Each group member had the responsibility to complete their tasks. Google Doc allows each group member to check and proofread their work and other group members as some tasks may overlap.

Discord - Discord is the main communication platform for the group. The group members are more active on Discord therefore any information, document, or files related to the assignment would be brought up on there. Information will also be sent to MS teams group chat in case a team member does not receive a notification on Discord

Microsoft Teams - MS Teams is primarily used for all of the group’s meetings and is a secondary platform for communication between group members. The meetings are held twice per week, which is every Wednesday and Saturday at 4:00 PM AEST.

Figma - Figma is a design and prototyping tool that was used for designing the wireframes and making the prototype of the project idea. It was used to design a mockup and the interactive prototype of the application

Figma Community - Usable data, tables, images were extracted from the Figma Community and use within Figma mockup and prototype of the app.

GitHub/GitHub Repository - Used to contribute to the group’s website. Each member is responsible for adding their part of the assignment to the repository.

Collaborate Ultra - Collaborate Ultra was used for consultations with Anthony Clapp. The consultation took place on the 5th and 12th of October at 13:05.

MS Outlook - To communicate with Anthony in regards to questions related to the assignment and to bring up the absence of Zhitai, the sixth group member.

W3 Schools - Use for learning how to use HTML and basic web development

YouTube - Used as a learning tool for various topics from Figma, Github Repository, HTML, and CSS Web Development.
